Storymap: The Holy Spear (Or Spear of Longinus)

This map follows the many travels of the famed "Spear of Longinus" which allegedly pierced Christ on the Cross. While there are five separate claims as to which one is the real spear, the map traces the historical locations of each claim along with a handy timeline to help gauge when these claims were made.
