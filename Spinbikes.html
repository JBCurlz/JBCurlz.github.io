Spinbikes on UK's Campus

This project, part of the introductory GEO109 course, was my first group mapping project. While full details of this project are covered in the video below, the goal of these maps was to examine where the rentable Spinbikes were being used most frequently around campus. The project only covered progression over a single day, but indicated that there was no need for Spin allocate more bikes to the campus area.
